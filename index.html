    FAQ

    • What’s included with each line?

      Sequence confirmation, basic phenotypic notes, and a datasheet. Additional assays available on request.

    • Can you target multiple genes?

      Yes. Multiplex designs are available for both Arabidopsis and tomato; lead times vary by complexity.

    • Do you provide seeds or plants?

      Both. Arabidopsis is commonly supplied as seeds or T1 plants; tomato as T1 plants.

    • Do you accept custom projects?

      Absolutely. Share targets, cultivar preferences, and constraints for a tailored quote.
